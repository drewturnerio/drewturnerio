# Code of Conduct

As a contributor and maintainer of this project, I pledge to foster an open and welcoming environment. I expect all contributors to adhere to the following:

* **Respect:** Treat all participants with respect and professional courtesy.
* **Collaboration:** Approach feedback with a constructive, learning-oriented mindset.
* **Safety:** Harassment or discriminatory language will not be tolerated.

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by these standards.
